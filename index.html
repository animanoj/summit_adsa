What is the Data Summit?

The ADSA Data Summit is a tech conference with emphasis on Big Data and Data Science, organized and run by the Association of Data Science and Analytics and the students at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

Who is going to be there?

We are going to bring students, speakers, and companies from across the country together, for a career fair, cool tech talks, multiple workshops, and the Data Hackathon! The conference is free and open to anyone regardless of age, major, job, or affiliation with the University of Illinois. Just show up and learn from the best and the brightest people in the industry!

Where is it?

The Data Summit will be held in the Siebel Center for Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. 201 N. Goodwin Ave, Urbana, IL

Data Hackathon

The Data Hackathon is a week long competition, where you will have the chance to play around with real world datasets to get hands on experience with data science. The data hackathon will have two challenges. Click the hyperlinks for the challenges to add yourself to the piazzas for the challenges.

The dataset, rules, and tasks for both the challenges are released. Make sure you add yourselves to the piazzas for your challenges!

Who is ADSA?

Association of Data Science and Analytics (ADSA) is a student organization at UIUC, focusing on data science and analytics. We are dedicated to preparing students for success in the fast-changing and data-driven future. We aim to train our members to be very best in the fields of data science and analytics throughout countless workshops, projects, and case studies. Check out what we are up to these days!

FAQ

Q. Do I need to register?

A. Nope! But you’ll get a free t-shirt if you do! Click here to register.

Q. Is the conference free?

A. Yes! The data summit is entirely free, which includes the career fair, talks, and the hackathon. So please come and have fun!

Q. How can I get involved?

A. If you are a student at UIUC, please keep an eye out for our Facebook Event and registration link. If you are a company, please feel free to email our Chair directly at rameshk2@illinois.edu.

Q. Will food be provided?

A. Did we mention free food for an entire weekend?
